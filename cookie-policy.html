Cookie Policy

Last updated: 27 October 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They store information about your browsing activity, preferences, and device details. Cookies are widely used to make websites work efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide personalized content.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Functionality: To ensure the website works correctly, remember your settings, and provide essential services.
  • Performance & Analytics: To understand how visitors use our website, monitor traffic, and identify areas for improvement.
  • Marketing & Advertising: To show relevant content and advertisements based on your interests, both on our website and across other platforms.
  • Security: To detect fraudulent activity and protect your data while using our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for the basic functioning of the website. They cannot be disabled and include cookies for login sessions, site navigation, and security.
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect information about how users interact with the website, including pages visited, time spent, and errors encountered. These cookies help improve performance and usability.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track user behavior to deliver personalized advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language, region, or layout settings to provide a more customized experience.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party service providers, such as:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) to track and report site usage.
  • Advertising networks to display personalized ads.
  • Social media platforms for sharing content and enabling social features.

These cookies are governed by the respective third-party privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they use your data.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookie preferences in multiple ways:

  • Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • Use the cookie consent banner to accept or reject non-essential cookies.
  • Some cookies may remain active if they are necessary for the website to function properly.

Disabling certain cookies may limit website functionality and prevent access to some services.

Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies according to this policy. You may change your consent preferences at any time through your browser settings or by interacting with our cookie banner.

Data Retention

Cookies have different lifespans. Some are session cookies that are deleted when you close your browser, while others are persistent and remain on your device for a set period. We ensure that we only keep cookies as long as necessary for their purpose.

Learn More About Cookies

Cookies help us provide a safer, faster, and more personalized browsing experience. They allow us to remember your preferences, login information, and the pages you visit. This enables us to tailor content, improve website performance, and provide relevant advertisements.

You have full control over cookies. You can accept all, reject non-essential cookies, or manage them individually through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect some features of the website, but necessary cookies will always remain active to ensure basic functionality.

For detailed information on specific cookies we use, their purpose, and duration, please see our Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Cookie Policy, please contact us at info@yourdomain.com. We are happy to provide further clarification or assist with cookie management.
