SME Loan

Small Medium Enterprise (SME) loans are designed to enable small entrepreneurs access business loans – working capital, project, supply (LPO) etc. Securities for these loans are based on traditional collaterals. The loan is to augment working capital and not for start-up business.

    Features

  • Competitive interest rate.
  • Flexible collateral.
  • Repayment is structured for every 30 days.
  • Business turnover determines the loan amount.
  • Tenor is within 180 days.

Back To School Loan

This loan facility is designed to support parents and guardians with the cost of school start-up — tuition, uniforms, supplies and other essentials — for the new academic term. It aims to ease the financial burden of back-to-school expenses and help families plan ahead.

    Features

  • The borrower’s net income or school fee requirement determines the loan amount.
  • Accessible to both public and private sector employees.
  • Seamless service delivery.
  • Convenient repayment schedule.
  • Flexible tenor.
