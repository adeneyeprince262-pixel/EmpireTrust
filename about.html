Who We Are

Empire Trust Microfinance Bank Limited was incorporated on 30th May 2014 (RC No. 1193892) with its registered office at 35 Oba Akran Avenue, Ikeja, Lagos State, Nigeria.

The Bank was licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria on 20th November 2014 to operate as a microfinance bank, committed to reducing the financial-inclusion gap and supporting individuals and SMEs.

In May 2019, the Bank’s Board approved a capital injection so that its capital base stood at N₦200 million, meeting the regulatory threshold for an Urban-Unit Microfinance Bank in Nigeria.

What We Do

Empire Trust provides a full suite of retail and SME financial services across Nigeria — designed to boost financial inclusion, support business growth and make everyday banking easier.

Loan Products

Working-capital and tailored loan solutions for individuals, salary earners and SMEs — structured to meet short- and medium-term financing needs.

  • Payday & payroll loans
  • SME working capital
  • Asset & school loans

Savings & Investments

Deposit products and savings plans to help customers grow funds safely.

  • Regular savings accounts
  • Fixed savings & investment plans
  • Group & corporate deposits

Digital Banking

Mobile, online and agent channels — enabling banking anytime, anywhere.

  • Mobile & internet banking
  • POS & USSD banking
  • ATM accessibility

Empire-Pay POS

Extending banking services to underserved communities nationwide.

  • POS terminal deployment
  • Deposits, withdrawals & transfers
  • Bill payments & mini statements

SME Support & Advisory

Business loans and support programs to help SMEs grow sustainably.

  • Business mentoring
  • Growth & expansion financing

Customer Support & Fast Onboarding

Simplified onboarding and responsive support for all customers.

  • Online & branch account opening
  • Dedicated service support

Our Story

Since inception in 2014, Empire Trust Microfinance Bank has remained dedicated to redefining financial accessibility. From our early beginnings in Lagos, we have grown to serve a broad range of clients with savings, loans, investments, and digital banking solutions.

With the introduction of our “Empire-pay” agent-banking solution, we continue to bring banking services to underserved communities, bridging traditional banking gaps with technology and trust.
