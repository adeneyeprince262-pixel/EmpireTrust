Empire Trust MFB Careers

A professional pursuit at Empire Trust MFB guarantees you an avenue for career growth, excellent culture, learning opportunities and a stimulating environment to spur you to success in your chosen career and ultimately contribute to the microfinance value chain. If you are ambitious, result-driven and innovative, a career with us will be a quantum leap in your endeavours.

Equal opportunities: Empire Trust MFB is fully committed to equal opportunities and non-discrimination on the basis of any legal status in the course of its recruitment and employment.

Why work with us

Career Growth

Structured learning, on-the-job training and internal promotion paths to help you grow professionally.

Inclusive Culture

A supportive environment that values teamwork, transparency and results-driven performance.

Impact

Work that directly supports SMEs and individuals — your work helps real people access finance and grow.
