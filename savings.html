Savings Account

Personal Savings

This account is designed for all categories of customers to encourage individual savings.

  • N500 minimum opening account requirement.
  • Friendly account opening process.
  • Free account maintenance fee.
  • Attractive interest rate.
  • Unlimited withdrawal.
  • SMS alert services.
  • Bank statement of account (available on request).
  • Debit card that works on all ATMs.
  • Deposit and withdrawal can be made via any of our correspondent bank branches.
  • Availability of mobile banking facility.

Savings Plus

A hybrid savings account that combines the flexibility of a current account with the benefits of a savings account.

  • N500 minimum opening account requirement.
  • Minimum account balance of N5,000.
  • SMS alert services.
  • Cheque/dividend warrant lodgement allowed.
  • Third party withdrawal.
  • Can be used to access loan facilities.
  • Free maintenance fee.
  • Cheques not valid for clearing.
  • Debit card that works on all ATMs.
  • Attractive interest rate.
  • Deposit and withdrawal can be made via any of our correspondent bank branches.
  • Availability of mobile banking facility.

Fixed Deposit

Yanga Deposit

Designed for both long and short-term investments, offering optimal returns to our customers.

  • Minimum deposit of N100,000.
  • Minimum annual interest of 12%.
  • Interest accruing on a daily basis and credited monthly.
  • Interest can be earned upfront.
  • Top-up is allowed.
  • Issuance of Debit card that works on any ATM.

Fixed Deposit

This product is designed for long and short term investment, Empire Trust MFB will provide the best options that guarantees optimal returns on investment to our different categories of customers.

  • Minimum fixed deposit of N50,000.
  • Part liquidation of investment allowed (Condtion applies).
  • Negotiable interest rate.
  • Minimum duration of 30 days.
  • Can be used as collateral to access loan.

Target Savings Deposit

Equity Savings

Encourages savings towards accessing loans and achieving financial milestones with Empire Trust MFB.

  • N5,000 minimum account opening requirement
  • After 6 months, customers can access up to 3x average monthly turnover
  • Savings can be made daily, weekly, or monthly
  • Minimum savings period of 6 months
  • Annual interest of 8%
  • Interest accruing daily and credited monthly
  • Issuance of Debit card that works on any ATM

Buga Savings

Encourages saving towards specific goals such as school fees, festive expenses, or projects.

  • Savings can be done daily, weekly, or monthly
  • Annual interest of 12%
  • Minimum duration of 30 days
  • Interest accrues daily and is credited monthly
  • Third withdrawal before 30 days leads to forfeiture of interest
